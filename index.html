Hamster Life at a Glance

bed Sleep & Comfort

The Cozy Nest

Hamsters are burrowers by nature. Providing at least 6 inches of soft, safe bedding allows them to create intricate tunnel systems and a warm sleeping nook.

water_drop

Hydration Station

Fresh water daily in a drip-free bottle is essential for a happy hammy.

restaurant

Pouch Power

Their cheek pouches can expand to hold a food stash as large as their head!

Did You Know?

Hamsters are crepuscular, meaning they are most active during twilight hours!

explore Activity

Adventure Time

Exploration keeps their minds sharp. Safe time in an exercise ball or a secured playpen is vital for their physical health.

spa

Sand Baths

A bowl of chinchilla sand helps keep their fur oil-free.
